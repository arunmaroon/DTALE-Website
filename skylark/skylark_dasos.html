OVERVIEW

Amidst six rolling acres on Hennur Road stands Skylark Dásos, an emerald retreat in the bustle of one of Bangalore's fastest developing areas. Dásos is named after the Greek word for 'forest', and true to its name, 80% of the property features a verdant green space, 50% of which is dedicated to tree cover. In the centre of this tropical paradise, will be three towers of 1, 2 and 3BHK apartments, surrounded by a host of world-class amenities that complete the Dásos experience.
