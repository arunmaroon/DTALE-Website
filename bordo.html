Product Design for an Ad OOH advertising aggregator platform to both media buyer and seller to interact and do the business with ease

Project Brief

To Design a platform to enhance the experience of dementia based carehomes and caregivers by enabling them to provide personalised, integrated, and empowering care.

An interactive tool to assist them with everyday tasks, and addressing the their emotional and physical stress.

Client

Bordo Tech

Target Audience

OOH buyers and sellers

Date

Feb-March 2017

Interactive Landing Page

How we Solved the Problem

OOH industry in India is still lagging behind in modernizing and have a poor utilization of resources. Modernization of OOH inventory management and through technology infusion.
Bordo users can view all OOH media listings in one place along with analytical information to make better decisions User friendly interface to facilitate ease of doing business

Wireframes

OOH media outlet sellers provide listing on Bordo.
Buyers reviews listings on Bordo and books an outlet with ease.
Buyers fees to seller is made through Bordo minus a nominal commission.

User Interfaces

Clarity of information and ease of use was on the highest priority. Using the brand colors visual elements and interactions were layed out to ease user flow.
